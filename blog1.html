How to Keep Your Hair Healthy

1.Regularly shampoo and condition your hair to remove dirt and excess oils.

2.Limit heat styling and use protective products when necessary to prevent damage.

3.Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote overall hair health.

Hair care routine

1.Shampoo and condition your hair every 2-3 days, using products formulated for your hair type.

2.Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle hair gently, starting from the ends and working your way up.

3.Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish and protect your hair from damage, and limit heat styling as much as possible.

Benefits of oiling hair

1.Oiling hair can help to nourish and moisturize the scalp, preventing dryness and flakiness

2.It can also help to strengthen hair, reducing breakage and promoting growth.

3.Massaging oil into the scalp can increase blood flow and promote relaxation, reducing stress and tension in the scalp and promoting healthy hair.
